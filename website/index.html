Features

Built to keep you steady

No perfect-week pressure. Steadily adapts to what actually happened and helps you show up again tomorrow.

Adaptive daily planning

Miss a task and Steadily reshuffles your plan around it, instead of guilting you into a backlog.

Habits & honest streaks

Streaks that reflect effort, not just perfect attendance — so one bad day doesn't erase your progress.

Project phases

Break big, vague goals into phases with a clear canvas view — no more one giant unmoving to-do list.

Insights that matter

See the patterns behind your habits and projects over weeks and months, not just today's checklist.

FAQ

Questions, answered

Steadily is an autonomous productivity planner. It plans your day, tracks your habits and streaks, and organizes bigger goals into projects with phases — and it adjusts automatically when your day doesn't go as planned.

Yes — the web app is free to use right now. If that changes for any future features, existing core planning and habit tracking will stay free.

An installable desktop app is in progress — see the Download section. Until it ships, the web app works fully in your browser and can be installed as a PWA on most systems.

Not automatically. Steadily is built around consistency, not perfection — streak logic accounts for real-life gaps instead of resetting on the first missed day.

Today, Steadily is focused on individual planning. Shared projects are on the roadmap — let us know if that's something you'd use.

Your data is stored under your account and isn't shared with third parties. You can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time from the app.
