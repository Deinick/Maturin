Features

Built to keep you steady

No perfect-week pressure. Steadily adapts to what actually happened and helps you show up again tomorrow.

Adaptive daily planning

Miss a task and Steadily reshuffles your plan around it, instead of guilting you into a backlog.

Habits & honest streaks

Streaks that count what you actually did — no padding, no fudged numbers, just a clear read on your consistency.

Project phases

Break big, vague goals into phases on a dependency canvas that shows what blocks what, with a milestone kanban board for the work inside each phase.

Insights that matter

Steadily flags what's actually going on — tasks you keep avoiding, milestones stalled for the same reason twice, and whether you're consistently over- or under-estimating effort.

Collaborative projects

Invite people by email as contributors or viewers — proposed changes go through review before they land, so shared projects stay in sync.

FAQ

Questions, answered

Steadily is an adaptive productivity planner. It plans your day, tracks your habits and streaks, and organizes bigger goals into projects with phases — and it adjusts automatically when your day doesn't go as planned.

Yes — the web app is free to use right now. If that changes for any future features, existing core planning and habit tracking will stay free.

An installable desktop app is in progress — see the Download section. Until it ships, the web app works fully in your browser and can be installed as a PWA on most systems.

Your streak counts consecutive completed days on that habit's active days, so missing one does reset it — there's no hidden grace period. What we won't do is pad the number or hide a miss to make it look better than it is. Your full history and stats stay intact either way, so you can always see the real pattern, not just today's count.

Yes. Invite people to a project by email and assign them a role — contributors or viewers. Contributors can propose changes to phases and milestones, and anyone with approval permission on the project reviews each proposed change before it's accepted or rejected, so nothing gets overwritten without a second set of eyes.

Your data is stored under your account and isn't shared with third parties. You can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time from the app.
